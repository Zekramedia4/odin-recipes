Curried Fruit

Description

This hot baked curried fruit can be served as a side dish or even dessert. It is a traditonal southern recipe, ready for fall or winter. The curry brings a unique depth of flavor and complexity to the fruit, while retaining sweetness (helped by the brown sugar of course). While canned fruit is typically used, feel free to use fresh, or even freestyle a bit with what fruits you choose to include.

Ingredients

  • Peaches
  • Pineapple
  • Pears
  • Maraschino Cherries
  • Apricots
  • 3/4c Brown Sugar
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • 3 tbsp Curry Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Spread fruit on towel and dry.
  3. Melt the butter, and mix together with the brown sugar and curry powder in a small bowl.
  4. Place fruit in a baking dish and pour the brown sugar mix over it.
  5. Bake for ~30 minutes.
