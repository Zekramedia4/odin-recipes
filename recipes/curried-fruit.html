Ingredients

  • Peaches
  • Pineapple
  • Pears
  • Maraschino Cherries
  • Apricots
  • 3/4c Brown Sugar
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • 3 tbsp Curry Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Spread fruit on towel and dry.
  3. Melt the butter, and mix with the brown sugar and curry powder in a small bowl.
  4. Place fruit in a baking dish and pour the brown sugar mix over it.
  5. Bake for ~30 minutes
